The tools

Your toolkit, grouped by job

Every tool shows real data and adds an AI read-out with concrete recommendations you can act on.

Track performance

GSC Dashboard

Your Google Search Console numbers — clicks, impressions, CTR and average position.

How: pick a site and date range, read the metrics, then hit Generate AI Insights for quick wins and cannibalization findings. You can even ask questions in plain English.

SEO Health

One health score (0–100) per client, pulled from your Looker Studio spreadsheets.

How: each client shows a score gauge, a trend sparkline and metrics by category. Click why? to see what's lowering the score. History fills in automatically day by day.

Tracking

uses credits

Watch keyword rankings over time and get alerted to changes.

How: add a keyword (with an optional page and location). It's re-checked automatically every day; open its history for the trend and watch the bell for drops, gains and snippet changes.

History

A log of past analyses so you can re-open results without re-running them.

How: filter by tool and click View on any run. Covers FS Stealer, SERP Analyzer and SEO Health.

Win rankings & snippets

SERP Analyzer

uses credits

A real Google results page for any keyword, broken down for you.

How: enter a keyword and optional location. See the organic results, related searches, People Also Ask, the featured snippet, and an AI read of the landscape. Saved to History.

FS Stealer

uses credits

A step-by-step, copy-paste plan to win the featured snippet for a keyword.

How: enter a keyword, your page URL and a location. You get a diagnosis, gap analysis vs. the current holder, an action plan and quick wins. Export it as a document, or click Track to monitor the keyword.

Audit your site

Technical Auditor

Site-health diagnostics in three tabs.

Core Web VitalsCrawl AuditLog Analysis

Core Web Vitals: real speed scores (mobile/desktop) with a history sparkline. Crawl Audit: upload a Screaming Frog crawl for an instant overview. Log Analysis: see how bots crawl your site, with a status/error/path breakdown.

On-Page Auditor

Page-level content quality — paste a URL into any tab.

HeadersSchemaImage AltE-E-A-T

How: check the heading structure, validate structured data, improve image alt text, and score the page against Google's quality (E-E-A-T) guidelines — each with fix-it suggestions.

Indexation Control

Are your pages actually in Google's index?

How: check individual URLs, pull a whole sitemap for coverage, or view an indexed-status timeline across days with PASS / FAIL coloring.

Links & comparison

Internal Linking

How your pages link to each other — and where they should.

Live scan: enter target URLs to see a link matrix, orphan pages and missing-link opportunities. Screaming Frog mode: upload an All Inlinks export to get a From/To/Anchor/Status table and an anchor-consistency check that flags the same anchor pointing to different pages.

URL Comparator

Your page vs. a competitor's, side by side.

How: enter two URLs and get an on-page comparison with an AI verdict on which is stronger and why.

FAQ

Common questions

The SEO Health or Core Web Vitals trend only shows one day.

Trends build up over time from a daily snapshot. Give it a few days, or click Refresh / re-analyze on multiple days to seed it faster.

A SERP or FS Stealer analysis said it failed.

Usually a temporary hiccup with the search source or a used-up credit for the day. Wait a minute and try again — the platform automatically falls back to alternate sources.

My big Screaming Frog file wouldn't upload before.

Internal Linking now reads CSV files right in your browser with no size limit. For very large crawls, export as CSV (not XLSX) and it'll load fine.

A page behind a login won't analyze.

Open the “🔒 Authentication” toggle in the tool and enter the staging username and password.

I can't see the Users menu.

That panel is for super-admins only. Ask an administrator if you need an account created or changed.
